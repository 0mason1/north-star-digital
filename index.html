How long does it take to build my site?
Most sites are ready to review within 48 hours of our first conversation. Once you approve it, it's live within 24 hours after that.
What if I don't like how it looks?
We revise it until you do. There's no limit on changes during the demo phase. You don't pay until you're completely happy.
What does it cost?
One flat setup fee and a straightforward monthly rate that covers hosting, updates, and support. We'll go over exact numbers on our first call with no pressure attached.
Do I need to provide anything?
Just your basic business info — phone number, address, services, and hours. We handle the rest, including sourcing professional photos if you don't have any.
What if I already have a website?
We can build you a better one and migrate everything over. If your current site is outdated or not bringing in customers, it's worth a conversation.
Can I cancel if I change my mind?
Yes. No long-term contracts. Month to month, cancel any time with no penalties. We keep clients by being worth keeping.
